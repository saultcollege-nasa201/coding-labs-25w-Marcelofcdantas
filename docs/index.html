TryHackme

Advantages

Disadvantages
  • Wide variety of hacking challenges, ideal for professionals.
  • Recognized by recruiters as a valuable skills assessment platform.
  • Interface may be less intuitive for beginners.
  • Steeper learning curve due to the complexity of challenges.

Hack The Box

Advantages

Disadvantages
  • Provides hands-on exercises and interactive labs.
  • User-friendly platform with guided tutorials, making it accessible for beginners.
  • Some advanced features may be limited in the free version.
  • Fewer complex challenges compared to Hack The Box.
